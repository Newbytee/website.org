The Kontalk Messaging Project

A new way of communicating.
What platforms are supported? Right now we have only an Android client. A web-based client is being developed and will be hopefully released soon; next would be iOS and Symbian platforms.
The Kontalk protocol is free and open source, if you are a developer you are free to write your own client!
Is the service for free? YES! And always will be. The service is driven by a community of volunteers (just like us, the founders of Kontalk) running their own cheap servers – which is fine by the way: the protocol is quite simple and requires few server resources.
Are the clients for free too? Yes, the clients we (the Kontalk team) develop are completely free. However, there could be a price for the iOS client because of the costs of the App Store. Besides, anyone could implement his/her own client and sell it – yes they can :-)
Why I need my phone number to register? Technically the Kontalk protocol supports any type of login name, but we prefer using phone numbers for two reasons:
  • we trust users having a real phone number, keeping spammers and bad guys out of the network
  • it helps keeping track of your Kontalk contacts list – it will contain just the contacts who actually have Kontalk installed (this is actually verified using phone numbers found in your contacts)
