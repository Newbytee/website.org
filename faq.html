The Kontalk Messaging Project

A new way of communicating.
What platform are supported?
Right now we have only an Android client. A cross-platform desktop client is being developed and will be released soon; next would be iOS and Symbian platforms.
The Kontalk protocol is free and open source, if you are a developer you are free to write your own client!
Is the service free?
YES! And always will be. The service is driven by a community of volunteers (just like us, the founders of Kontalk) running their own cheap servers – which is fine by the way: the protocol is quite simple and requires few server resources.
Are the clients free too?
Yes, the clients we (the Kontalk team) develop are completely free. Although, others could implement their own client and sell it – yes they can :-)
Why I need my phone number to register?
Technically the Kontalk protocol supports any type of login name, but we prefer using phone numbers for two reasons:
  • we trust users who have a real phone number, keeping spammers and bad guys out of the network
  • it helps keeping track of the Kontalk contacts list - it will contain just the contacts who actually have Kontalk installed; this is actually verified using phone numbers found in your contacts
